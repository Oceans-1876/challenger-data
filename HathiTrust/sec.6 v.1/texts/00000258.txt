THE VOYAGE OF H.M.S. CHALLENGER.
STATION 22.
At 9.30 A.M. shortened and furled sails, and got up steam to sound. Sounded in
1420 fathoms. At noon obtained a series of temperatures at intervals of 100 fathoms
down to the bottom. At 12.20 P.M. put trawl over and veered 2400 fathoms. At 2 P.M.
jollyboat with naturalists away 'collecting surface animals. At 5.15 P.M. commenced
heaving in trawl, which came up at 8 P.M. with the beam broken ; it contained a small
black fish.
Distance at noon from Sombrero Island, 30 miles. Made good 84 miles. Amount
of current 6 miles, direction N. 62° W.

ORGANISMS FROM
SURFACE-NETS.
Surface Organisms.-Large quantities of Oscillatoriaceæ are recorded from the surface,
and naturalists in a boat captured some pelagic Actiniæ (Plotactis ?), and three young
Plectognathous fish (Tetrodon).
