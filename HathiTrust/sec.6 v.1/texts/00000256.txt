STATION 19.
Station 19 (Sounding 63), Tenerife to Sombrero (see Chart 6 and Diagram 1).
March 11, 1873 ; lat. 19° 15' N., long. 57° 47' W.
Temperature of air at noon, 77°:8; mean for the day, 76°.5.
Temperature of water at surface, 75°:0; bottom, 35°•5.
Density at 60° F. at surface, 1.02728; bottom, 1.02614.
Depth, 3000 fathoms ; deposit, Red Clay, containing 1.49 per cent. of carbonate of
lime (see Murray and Renard, Deep-Sea Deposits Chall. Exp.).
At 3 P.M. sounded in 3000 fathoms, and at 4.30 P.m. made all plain sail. Flying-
fish were very abundant, and gulf-weed floated past in considerable quantity.
Sombrero Island distant at noon, 344 miles. Made good 132 miles. Amount of
current 16 miles, direction N. 86° W.
