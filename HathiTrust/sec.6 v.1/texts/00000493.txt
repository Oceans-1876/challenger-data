In addition to the foregoing, the following are recorded in the Station-book :-
Fragments of Chirodota, Synapta, Nemertean, Caridid shrimp, and small fish.
Excluding Protozoa, about 20 specimens of invertebrates and fishes were obtained
on this date, belonging to about 15 species, of which 8 are now to science, including
representatives of 4 new genera; 5 of the new species and 1 new genus were not
obtained elsewhere.
