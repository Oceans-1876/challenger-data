Density at 60° F. at surface, 1.02699 ; 150 fathoms, 1.02662; bottom, 1.02688.
Depth, 900 fathoms; deposit, Pteropod Ooze, containing 52-22 per cent. of
carbonate of lime (see Murray and Renard, Deep-Sea Deposits Chall. Exp.).
At 9.15 A. M. shortened and furled sails, and got up steam to sound and dredge. Sounded
in 900 fathoms. Put over dredge. At 2 P.M. obtained temperatures at intervals of
100 fathoms down to the bottom. Hove up dredge with some specimens, and put it over
again. At 4.15 P.M. hove up dredge, which contained a few specimens. At 6 P.M. com-
pleted temperatures, and at 7.30 P.M. made all plain sail.
Distance from St. Michael's at noon, 67 miles.
