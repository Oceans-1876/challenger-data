AT TERNATE.
The Expedition remained at Ternate from 6 P.M. on October 14 till 11 A.M. on
October 17, 1874 (for description of Ternate see Narr. Chall. Exp., vol. i. pp. 595–600).
The following species are recorded in the Zoological Reports as having been obtained in
the vicinity :
