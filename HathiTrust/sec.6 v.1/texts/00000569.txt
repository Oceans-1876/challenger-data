Two hauls of the tow-net were taken between 8 and 12 P.m., and procured
Saphirinid, Hyperia, Zoëæ (much larger than on the previous day), other Decapod larvæ,
Atlanta, Styliola, Cleodora [ = Clio), Salpa (in catena, very numerous). In the stomachs
of the Salpæ were many Coccospheres and a few Rhabdospheres. There was little
phosphorescence at the surface.
