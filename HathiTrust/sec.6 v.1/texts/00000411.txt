Bugula mirabilis, n.sp. Several specimens ; obtained at no other locality.

Recorded subsequently from New England (U.S. Fish Com-
mission)
The Station-book records also : Trunk of Pennatulid, red Holothurian with Stylifer
in its cloaca, and two specimens of Scalpellum on a Polyzoon.
Excluding Protozoa, about 20 specimens of invertebrates and fishes were ob-
tained at this Station, belonging to about 12 species, of which 9 are new to science,
including representatives of 3 new genera; 7 of the new species were not obtained
elsewhere.
