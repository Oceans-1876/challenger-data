Surface Organisms.—The following are recorded in the note-books as having been ORGANISMS FROM
SURFACE-VETS.
captured in the surface tow-nets :
Acanthometra, Collosphæra ; Physalia, Rhizophysa filiformis (?), Diphyes, Cuboides ;
Calanella [ = Eucalanus), Hyalophyllum [= Saphirinella], Corycæus, Hyperia,
Lucifer, Zoëæ ; Styliola, Hyalæa [ Cavolinia); Appendicularia, Doliolum.
Moseley writes : “A large Physalia was caught, and I obtained good preparations of
the cnidæ by placing one of the nematophorous threads at full stretch on a glass slip and
allowing it to dry there. As the thread dried all the cells shot their threads, and these
latter formed a fluffy margin to the dried mass plainly visible to the naked eye. By
drying a thread rapidly over a spirit-lamp preparations were obtained in which the threads
were not ejected.”
