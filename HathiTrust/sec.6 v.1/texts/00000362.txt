At night large quantities of luminous animals were observed in the water, and being
nearly calm great numbers were caught in the tow-net, which was hauled in four times
during the night. There were large specimens of Alciopa 5 dcm. in length and 6 mm.
in breadth, Tomopteris, Sagitta, Cypris-like larva of a Cirriped, many Amphipods,
especially Phronima and Hyperia, the Euphauside which have often been caught in
great quantities, Lucifer, Halobates, many specimens of Atlanta, Cleodora ( = Clio),
Styliola, and Cuvierina.
