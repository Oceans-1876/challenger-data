Excluding Protozoa, about 150 specimens of invertebrates and fishes were obtained
at this Station, belonging to about 78 species, of which 44 are new to science, including
representatives of 7 new genera ; 28 of the new species and 4 of the new genera (each
represented by a single species) were not obtained elsewhere.
The following species of Pteropoda, Heteropoda, Foraminifera, and Radiolaria, were
