THE VOYAGE OF H.M.S. CHALLENGER.
STATIONS 174
TO 174D.
At Station 174c, 610 fathoms, serial temperatures were taken down to the bottom. At
4.30 P.M. proceeded into Ngaloa Harbour, anchoring at 5.45 P.M. in 11% fathoms.
ANIMALS FROM
TRAWL AND
DREDGE.
The following species are recorded in the Zoological Reports from the trawl and
dredge at these Stations, the depth being indicated :-
MONAXONIDA (Ridley and Dendy, Zool. pt. 59).
Chondrocladia clavata, n.sp. Two specimens (610 fathoms); obtained at no other
locality. [Recorded in error from 140 fathoms).
