In addition to the foregoing, the following are recorded in the Station-book :-Act-
inia, many specimens of an unknown Urchin, very large specimen of Sipunculus.
Excluding Protozoa, over 50 specimens of invertebrates were obtained at this
Station, belonging to about 33 species, of which 17 are new to science, including re-
presentatives of 7 new genera; 6 of the new species and 1 new genus were not obtained
elsewhere.
