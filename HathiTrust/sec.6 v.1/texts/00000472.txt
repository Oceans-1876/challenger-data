4.20 P.M. furled sails, and sounded in 500 fathoms, deposit Red Mud, containing

which contained a few specimens, and proceeded under steam. At midnight sounded
in 60 fathoms, no bottom.
Distance at noon from Cape San Antonio, Bahia, 360 miles. Made good 57 miles.
Amount of current 24 miles, direction N. 19° W.
