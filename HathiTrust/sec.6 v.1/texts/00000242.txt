Density at 60° F. at surface, 1.02753; bottom, 1.02744.
Depth, 2740 fathoms; deposit, Red Clay, containing 12.00 per cent. of carbonate
of lime (see Murray and Renard, Deep-Sea Deposits Chall. Exp.).
At 2.15 P.M. proceeded under steam, head to wind as necessary for sounding, and
sounded at 3 P.m. in 2740 fathoms. At 5 P.M. put over dredge, with 2 cwt. attached
400 fathoms from the dredge, and at 7.20 P.M. veered dredge-rope to 3400 fathoms. At
1.15 A.m. next morning commenced heaving in dredge, which came up at 5.45 A.M. full
of Red Clay.
Position at noon, about 500 miles south-west of Tenerife ; Sombrero Island distant
2220 miles. Made good 144 miles. Amount of current 14 miles, direction S. 78° W.
