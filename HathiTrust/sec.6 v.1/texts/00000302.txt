AT BERMUDA.
The Challenger remained at Bermuda from 5 P.M. on April 4, till 7 A.M. on April 21.
April 17, 1873.
Sounding taken from boat, about a mile from the reef at Bermuda.
Depth, 200 fathoms ; deposit, Coral Sand, containing 93:34 per cent. of carbon-
ate of lime (see Murray and Renard, Deep-Sea Deposits Chall. Exp.).
