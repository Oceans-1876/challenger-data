700 fathoms. At 5.45 P. M. the dredge was hauled on board containing several specimens.
At this time the ship was close to the island, and for a moment the clouds, which had
veiled the summit for the last few days, opened and revealed the whole island of Tristan
da Cunha from its base to its snow-clad peak; a sketch of the same was obtained.
At 6.15 P.M. made all plain sail and proceeded towards the Cape, with a favourable
breeze from the N.W. Temperature of air at noon, 51°:8; mean for the day, 51°:6.
