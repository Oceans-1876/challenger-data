In addition, the following are recorded in the note-books :—Hydromedusa (Oceania),
small Planarians, small Tomopterids, Peltidium, Calanids and other Copepods,
Gammarus and another Amphipod, small Isopod, Zoëæ (probably of the Brachyurous
crab inhabiting the pools) very small and having just left the eggs. On the
floating masses of Macrocystis were found Foraminifera, Hydroids, Holothurians, small
bivalve shells, Patella, and Polyzoa. Occasionally the tow-net was completely filled
with various species of Diatoms, at other times with Amphipods (Hyperia) and
numerous Copepods ; Pteropods (Limacina) were also at times very
abundant.
