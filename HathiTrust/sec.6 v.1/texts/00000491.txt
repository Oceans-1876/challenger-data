In addition to the foregoing, the Station-book records also :—Small Tisiphonia,

Excluding Protozoa, about 20 specimens of invertebrates and fishes were obtained
at this Station, belonging to about 11 species, of which 6 are new to science, including
representatives of 2 new genera.
