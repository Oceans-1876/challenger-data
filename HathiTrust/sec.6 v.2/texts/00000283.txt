Glaucus atlanticus, Forster (infested by Distoma glauci).
In addition, the following are recorded in the note-books :-Foraminifera very
abundant, especially Pullenia obliquiloculata, while Globigerina, Sphæroidina, and
Pulvinulina were also numerous ; Sagitta, Copepods, Hyperids, larvæ of Euphausia,
Sergestes, Lucifer, Pteropods, and Appendicularia. Long banks of compound Radiolaria
were passed through, and several large Salpæ were seen. There was a good deal of
phosphorescence in the wake of the ship.
