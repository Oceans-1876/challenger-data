LAMELLIBRANCHIATA (Smith, Zool. pt. 35).
Silenia sarsii, n.g., n.sp. One specimen ; obtained also at Station 157. Only

GASTEROPODA (Watson, Zool. pt. 42).
Stylifer brychius, n.sp. Several specimens ; obtained at no other locality.
