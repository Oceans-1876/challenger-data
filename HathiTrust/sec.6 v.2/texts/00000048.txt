ANIMALS FROM
TRAWL AND
DREDGE.
The following species are recorded in the Zoological Reports from the trawl and
dredge at this Station :--
TETRACTINELLIDA (Sollas, Zool. pt. 63).
Myriastra clavosa, Ridley, var. quadrata, nov. One specimen; the species
obtained also at Stations 186 and 208.
ALCYONARIA (Wright and Studer, Zool. pt. 64).
Spongodes florida (Esper). One specimen; obtained at no other locality by the
Challenger. Recorded from Australia and Philippines.
anguina, n.sp. One specimen ; obtained at no other locality.
One young specimen; obtained also in Torres Strait.
bicolor, n.sp.
PENNATULIDA (Kölliker, Zool. pt. 2).
Pteroeides esperi, Herklots. Several specimens; obtained at no other locality by
the Challenger. Recorded from Philippines.
Halisceptrum gustavianum, Herklots, var. parvifolia, Külliker. Four specimens ;
obtained at no other locality by the Challenger.
Scytalium sarsi, Herklots. Five specimens; obtained at no other locality by
the Challenger. Original locality unknown.
CORALS (Moseley, Zool. pt. 7).
Heterocyathus philippinensis, Semper. Numerous specimens; obtained at
other locality by the Challenger.
Heteropsammia michelini, M.-Edwards and Haime. Numerous specimens ;
obtained at no other locality by the Challenger.
CRINOIDEA (Carpenter, Zool. pt. 60).
Antedon milberti (Müller). Two specimens; obtained also at Station 203.

anceps, n.sp. Three specimens ; obtained at no other locality.
quinduplicava, n.sp. One specimen and fragment; obtained at no
other locality.
