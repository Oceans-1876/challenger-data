THE VOYAGE OF H.M.S. CHALLENGER.
STATION 233B.
Excluding Protozoa, over 80 specimens of invertebrates and fishes were obtained
at this Station, belonging to about 32 species, of which 6 are new to science;
3 of the new species were not obtained elsewhere.

At 7.30 A.M. got up steam. At 9.40 A.M. weighed anchor and proceeded under steam
towards Bingo Nada. At 11.15 A.M. passed Hyakfu Kung Island. At 12.15 P.M. stopped
to traw) and obtain serial temperatures. The first haul of the trawl was unsuccessful, but
at 1.15 P.M. the trawl came up for the second time with many specimens. At 1.30 P.M.
proceeded. At 2.30 P.M. passed Matsu Sima. At 3.15 P.M. stopped and anchored in
9 fathoms off Sanagi Sima, at entrance to channel leading to Harima Nada.

TRAWL.

this Station :
