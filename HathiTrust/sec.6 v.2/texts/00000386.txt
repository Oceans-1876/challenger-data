THE VOYAGE OF H.M.S. CHALLENGER.
STATION 299.
Excluding Protozoa, over 200 specimens of invertebrates and fishes were obtained
at this Station, belonging to about 61 species, of which 45 are new to science,
including representatives of 19 new genera; 20 of the new species and 3 new genera
were not obtained elsewhere.
