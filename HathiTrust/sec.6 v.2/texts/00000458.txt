In addition, the following are recorded in the note-books :-Peridinium tripos,
Globigerina, Acanthometra and compound Radiolaria, Medusæ, Ctenophoræ, Echinoderm
larvæ, Hyperids, Phronima, larvæ of Euphausia. The stomachs of the Salpæ contained
Coccospheres, Diatoms, and Peridinium. It was curious to note the absence of the small
red Copepods so abundant on the previous day, their place being taken by several species
of Saphirina and a few blue Copepods. Sharks and flying fishes were seen.
