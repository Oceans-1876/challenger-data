At 1.30 P. M. stopped and took a series of temperatures down to 300 fathoms, about
30 miles from the 100-fathom line off the Bijouga Islands; depth indicated on Admiralty
Chart, 680 fathoms. At 2.50 P.M. proceeded under steam. Several Thalassidromas were
seen, and on the evening of the 15th a swallow flew about the ship.
Cape Verde distant at noon, 237 miles ; Bijouga Breakers, 66 miles N.E.
