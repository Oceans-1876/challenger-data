Station 265.

At 4.30 A.M. shortened and furled sails, and got up steam to sound and dredge. At
5.30 A.M. stopped and sounded in 2900 fathoms. At 7 A.M. put dredge over and veered
3600 fathoms. Obtained serial temperatures down to 1500 fathoms. At 1 P.M.
commenced heaving in dredge, which came up at 4.30 P.M. filled with a fine tenacious
ooze and with one mutilated fish. Made all plain sail. A gannet and a prion-like bird
were seen.
Venus Point, Tahiti, distant at noon, 1820 miles. Made good 27 miles. Amount
of current for the last two days 37 miles, direction S. 71° W.
