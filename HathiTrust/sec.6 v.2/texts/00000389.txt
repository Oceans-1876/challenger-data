STATION 299.
CIRRIPEDIA (Hoek, Zool. pt. 25).
Scalpellum darwinii, n.sp. One specimen (attached to manganese nodule); obtained
at no other locality.
PYCNOGONIDA (Hoek, Zool. pt. 10).
Nymphon procerum, n.sp. One specimen ; obtained at no other locality.
SCAPHOPODA (Watson, Zool. pt. 42).
Dentalium keras, n.sp. One specimen ; obtained also at Station 246. Recorded
subsequently from North Atlantic (“ Blake").
POLYZOA (Busk, Zool. pt. 30).
Menipea pateriformis, n.sp. Obtained at no other locality.
Bugula reticulata, n.sp. Obtained also at Stations 147, 303, and 320.
Kinetoskias pocillum, n.sp.
Obtained also at Station 122.
Flustra biseriata, n.sp.
Obtained also at Station 196.
BRACHIOPODA (Davidson, Zool. pt. 1).
Terebratula wyvillii, n.sp. Two specimens; obtained also at Stations 160, 184,
244, 302, and 317.
Waldheimia wyvillii, n.sp. One specimen ; obtained at no other locality.
TUNICATA (Herdman, Zool. pts. 17 and 76).
Corynascidia suhmi, n.g., n.sp. One specimen ; obtained also at Station 146.
Only species of the genus.
Octacnemus bythius, Moseley, n.g., n.sp. One specimen ; obtained also at Station 218.
Only species of the genus.
FISHES (Günther, Zool. pt. 57).
Macrurus filicauda, n.sp. One specimen ; obtained also at Stations 146, 157, 158,
323, and 325.
In addition to the foregoing, the following are recorded in the Station-book :-Several
specimens of Stephanoscyphus, Hydroid, several Serpulæ, Arcturus (?), Fusus with
Epizoanthus, several specimens of Discina, and single valve of Pleuronectia (?). The
Polyzoa, Discina, Stephanoscyphus, and Serpulæ were attached both to the manganese
nodules and to the Holothurians.
(SUMMARY OF RESULTS CHALL. EXP.--1893.)
