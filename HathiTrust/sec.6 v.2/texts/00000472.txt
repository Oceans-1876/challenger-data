In addition to the foregoing, the following are recorded in the Station-book :-
Actinian attached to Dentalium, Hydroid attached to Rhizocrinus stem, four Holothurians
of one species, and Arenicola (?). The netting of the trawl was covered with the
branching Rhizopod Rhizammina algæformis.
Excluding Protozoa, about 80 specimens of invertebrates and fishes were obtained
at this Station, belonging to about 34 species, of which 25 are new to science, including
representatives of 7 new genera; 14 of the new species and 1 new genus were not
obtained elsewhere.
