In addition, the following are recorded in the note-books :-Globigerina, Orbulina,
Medusæ, Siphonophoræ, Distomum, Cypridina, Copepods, Cypris stage of Lepas
fascicularis, Archizoëa, Hyperids, Phronima, Euphausia, Sergestes (?), Halobates,
Nudibranchs (on dead Velellæ), Atlanta, Oxygyrus, and other Heteropods and larvæ,
Pteropods, Salpa, fish eggs and egg-capsules (on dead Velellä). Two tow-nets were
sent down on the trawl-line—one at the trawl and one at the weights; the former was
completely torn, but the latter contained Phæodaria and other Radiolaria, as well as
some Crustacea not observed on the surface. A shark was seen about the ship.
