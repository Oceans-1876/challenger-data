ANIMALS FROM
TRAWL.
The following species are recorded in the Zoological Reports from the trawl at this
Station :
OPHIUROIDEA (Lyman, Zool. pt. 14).
Ophiochytra epigrus, n.g., n.sp. One specimen ; obtained at no other locality.
Only species of the genus.
OSTRACODA (Brady, Zool. pt. 3).
Cythere circumdentata, n.sp. Obtained also at Station 246.
1
MACRURA (Spence Bate, Zool. pt. 52).
Aristeus armatus, n.sp. One specimen ; obtained also at Stations 133, 184, 213,
237, 246, and 323.
Benthesicymus crenatus, n.g., n.sp. Two specimens ; obtained also at Station 272.
FISHES (Günther, Zool. pt. 57).
Malacosarcus macrostoma, n.g., n.sp. One specimen ; obtained also at Station 271.
Only species of the genus.
The Station-book records also Stephanoscyphus on the manganese nodules.
Excluding Protozoa, about 10 specimens of invertebrates and fishes were obtained
at this Station, belonging to 6 species, of which 5 are new to science, including
representatives of 3 new genera; 1 new genus and new species was not obtained
elsewhere.
