THE VOYAGE OF H.M.S. CHALLENGER.
The following 85 species, taken by the Challenger to the south of the tropics, are
recorded from other sources to the north of the tropics, and, as far as can be gathered
from the Challenger Zoological Reports, are not known from the intervening tropical
zone :-
