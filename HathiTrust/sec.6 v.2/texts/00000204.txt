In addition to the foregoing, the following are recorded in the Station-book :—Three
Actinians, several specimens of Stephanoscyphus on pumice-stones, three Ophiurids
(besides Ophioglypha), four Asteroidea belonging to three species (only one given
above), two Polyzoons, and another Brachiopod (besides Discina).
Excluding Protozoa, about 150 specimens of invertebrates and fishes were obtained
at this Station, belonging to about 48 species, of which 38 are new to science,
including representatives of 6 new genera ; 19 of the new species were not obtained
elsewhere.
