The tow-nets procured Oscillatoriaceæ in great abundance, Pyrocystis, Diatoms,
Peridinium, a few Coccospheres and Rhabdospheres, Globigerina, Orbulina, Pullenia,
Sphæroidina, Pulvinulina, Medusæ, Siphonophoræ, Sagitta, Alciopa, Tomopteris,
Aphroditacean larvæ, Copepods, Hyperids, Decapods and larvæ, Atlanta and other
Heteropods, various species of Pteropods, Appendicularia, Doliolum, and small fishes.
A tow-net sent down to 200 fathoms yielded Challengeridæ and other deep-sea Radiolaria.
