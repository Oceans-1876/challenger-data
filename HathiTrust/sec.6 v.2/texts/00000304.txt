The tow-nets procured mostly the same organisms as at the last few Stations,
including :-Pyrocystis, Diatoms, many Globigerina, Orbulina, Acanthometræ and other
Radiolaria, Copepods, Lucifer, and other Crustacea, small cuttle-fish. One of the
Phæodaria (Conchopsis) was observed alive under the microscope ; it threw out
pseudopodia in the same manner as the Foraminifera. The large brown cells of the
phæodium were turned round and round by the motions of the pseudopodia, which
attached themselves to the glass and moved the whole shell.
