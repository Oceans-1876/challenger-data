THE VOYAGE OF H.M.S. CHALLENGER.
PHILIPPINES.
LAMELLIBRANCHIATA (Smith, Zool. pt. 35).
Perna samoensis, Baird. (Samboangan, 10 fathoms); obtained also at Sandwich
Islands. Recorded from Samoa and Keeling Islands.
Pecten lemniscatus, Reeve (?). One specimen (Samboangan, 10 fathoms); obtained
at no other locality by the Challenger.
GASTEROPODA (Watson, Zool. pt. 42).
Haliotis varia, Linné. (Samboangan, 10 fathoms); obtained at no other locality
by the Challenger. Recorded from East Africa to Australia.
Trochus (Chrysostoma) paradoxus, Born. (Samboangan, 10 fathoms); obtained
also at Admiralty Islands. Recorded from Mauritius,
Nicobars, Philippines, and New Caledonia.
Nerita chamæleon, Linné. (Samboangan, 10 fathoms); obtained at no other
locality by the Challenger. Recorded from Indian Ocean
and Malay Archipelago.
Murex (Phyllonotus) endivia, Lamarck. (Zebu, shore); obtained also at Station 208.
Voluta (Vespertilio) vespertilio, Linné, var. pellis serpentis, Lamarck. (Samboangan,
10 fathoms); obtained at no other locality by the Challenger.
Recorded from Indian Ocean, Moluccas, and Papua.
Conus (Cylindrella) sulcatus, Hwass. (Zebu, shore); obtained at no other locality
by the Challenger. Recorded from China, East Indies, and
Indian Ocean,
Cypræa cylindrica, Born. (Samboangan, 10 fathoms); obtained at no other locality
by the Challenger. Recorded from Indian and Pacific Oceans.
POLYPLACOPHORA (Haddon, Zool. pt. 43).
Schizochiton incisus (Sowerby). Three specimens (Samboangan) ; obtained at no
other locality by the Challenger. Recorded from Australia
and Philippines.
Cryptoplax oculatus (Quoy and Gaimard). (Samboangan, 10 fathoms); obtained at
no other locality by the Challenger. Recorded from New
Guinea and Vanikoro.
NUDIBRANCHIATA (Bergh, Zool. pt. 26).
Ceratosoma cornigerum (Adams), var. (?). One specimen (Samboangan, 10 fathoms);
obtained at no other locality by the Challenger.
Recorded from Indian Ocean.
